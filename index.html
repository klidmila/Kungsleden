Food / Shop stock

Key difference: Large vs Small

Both shop sizes guarantee the core resupply staples all season (chocolate, hard bread, cheese, canned veg, sausage/meat, freeze-dried meals, powder soup, hygiene basics). The differences: in Large, loose-weight pasta/rice/lentils are guaranteed, but packaged 500g pasta is not (stock-dependent). In Small it's the reverse: packaged 500g pasta is guaranteed, but loose pasta/rice/lentils are stock-dependent. Small also simply lacks whole categories that Large may carry: salami, dried game sausage, olives, nuts, tortillas, parmesan, olive oil, dark rye bread.
